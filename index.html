While attending college at Colgate University I began my initial foray into the tech world, founding a company called LiveHyve that built a group-focused social media platform. I subsequently co-founded a company called InterAction Education that built an award-winning app called Math Evolve, which gamified math drills for elementary school students.

Through these entrepreneurial experiences I discovered a passion for creating and began to delve into the world of coding, which led me to enroll in Galvanize's Full-Stack Web Development Immersive program. I am particularly drawn to front-end web development and design, as I enjoy creating intuitive user interfaces that make for an elegant user experience. When I'm not writing code, I am an ardent yoga practitioner and I enjoy hiking and camping.
